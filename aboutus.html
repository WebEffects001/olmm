about us


who we are

OLMM Consulting Engineers is a highly regarded and well-established structural engineering firm with offices in Oakland and San Francisco. We provide complete structural design, seismic analysis, and retrofit design services to both public and private sector clients. Firm is highly disciplined and has always prided itself in meeting schedules and bringing in projects within established budgets. Our solutions to structural challenges are not only imaginative and innovative but also practical and cost-effective.

OLMM is a California 'C' Corporation and has been in business since 1986.



Badri Prasad

Principal / President

Mr. Prasad has over 30 years of experience leading design teams for the structural engineering of major new buildings, including healthcare facilities, courts, airports, schools, and seismic retrofits. His areas of expertise include the structural design of complex buildings of all types and materials, seismic rehabilitation of existing buildings, non-linear static push-over analyses, design execution, and project management. He has expertise with mixed construction types and materials, and is experienced with performance-based engineering, design, and rehabilitation for enhanced seismic performance levels. He has published and presented papers at several seminars and is also one of the authors of the design guide for the Design of Diaphragms, Chords and Collectors, published by National Council of Structural Engineers Association (NCSEA).



Tomasz Wienskowski

Associate Principal

Tomasz is a California licensed professional structural engineer with over 20 years in structural design. Design work includes Education Sector under the Division of the State Architect (DSA), Healthcare work including California’s Office of Statewide Health Planning and Development (OSHPD) projects, cable structures, base isolation, earthquake evaluations, property loss consulting, and blast protection design. Tomasz is a resourceful leader with the ability to communicate with personnel on all levels inside and outside of the organization.



Mallikarjuna (Mallik) Doddupla

Associate

Mallik is an Associate at OLMM Consulting Engineers with more than 25 years of structural analysis, design and construction administration experience. Mallik has particular expertise in structural analysis and design of office and high-rise residential buildings. He has also worked on numerous multiple housing/mixed use projects located throughout San Francisco and the Bay Area.



values


We pride ourselves on sustaining a multitude of core company values:

Client Centric: Clients are the center of what we do. We service a variety of public and private sector clients whose requirements, from budget constraints to time sensitivity, are of the utmost importance.

"OLMM's attention to design coordination and detail was readily apparent by the lack of any significant structural challenges during construction.

Vice President
Clark Construction


Innovative: OLMM remains at the forefront of using the latest technology such as performance-based design, passive energy dissipating system such as base-isolation and buckling restrained braces. We have received a number of industry awards for our structural solutions. Innovation includes constant growth, which is why we are committed to incorporating sustainability in our designs, exploring methods of maximizing recycled materials. Our efforts have resulted in LEED certifications on multiple projects.

Responsive: We are highly creative and responsive to shifting client needs. We strongly believe in prompt and timely response to client’s questions and needs - this has resulted in repeat clients over the years.

Experienced: We have a combined 75+ years of industry experience on our leadership team and have worked on a diverse array of both Design-Bid-Build and Design-Bid projects, from healthcare, education and transportation facilities. Our staff has high level of familiarity with Revit and BIM capability.

Cost-effective and Practical Solutions: OLMM has a strong record of bringing in projects on time and within the established budget. Our approach prioritizes practical structural solutions without sacrificing innovation or creativity.
